Data Science | Data Stories | Data Products

I have 3+ years experience building end-to-end credit risk models for assessing new business risk and behavior risk in the agritech and fintech sector across Africa.
I work remotely as a Data Analytics Scientist with Superfluid Labs, a Pan-African data analytics company with teams in Ghana, Kenya and Berlin.
My unique work experience gives me the added advantage of working successfully with teams of data engineers, data scienctists, business analysts, project managers across different timezones, understanding how to manage stakeholder expectations, identifying and communicating actionable insights and recommendations of business value and impact to stakeholders as well as identifying potential risks to project success and taking strategic steps to mitigate such risks.
I hold a Bachelors degree in Actuarial Science from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Ghana and a Masters degree in Applied Statistics from Loyola University Chicago
